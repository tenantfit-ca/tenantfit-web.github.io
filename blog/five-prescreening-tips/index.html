5 Screening Tips to Start With Today

While every rental situation is unique, here are a few tips that can help you get started screening tenants more efficiently:

  1. Ask about their move-in timeline. Knowing when they need to move helps you prioritize serious candidates who fit your availability.
  2. Ask if they have rented before. Prior renting experience often indicates familiarity with tenant responsibilities. If they did not you can plan for a more thorough onboarding.
  3. Be consistent: Send the exact same form and questions for all candidates to ensure fairness.
  4. Document decisions: Keep a separate folder per candidate where you take notes about your conversation and all related documents.
